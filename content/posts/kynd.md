## Lead UX/UI Designer from 2020-2026

Led end-to-end UX/UI design for desktop/mobile app at Kynd, evolving from startup to acquisition. Mapped user journeys, crafted mockups, and collaborated with product, founder, marketing, and tech teams to define app look, feel, and behaviour.

All Information shown is mockup only.

# Key Contributions
+ Led interface design from initial ideation to the current active product.
+ Designed UI/UX for both customer-facing applications and internal support tools.
+ Worked in a collaborative environment with frequent communication between teams and leadership.
+ Maintained and updated mockups and user journeys to ensure consistency.
+ Conducted QA and testing with developers to align builds with the product vision.
+ Used data-driven iteration post-launch to improve engagement and usability.
+ Wore multiple hats across QA, marketing, community support, product management, technical support, and documentation.

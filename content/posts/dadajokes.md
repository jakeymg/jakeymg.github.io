## 48hr Global Game Jam 2024

Teammate and I did a rapid ideation process to find a concept we both liked, then split the tasks. I created art assets while my teammate coded. Later integrated UI and assets into Unity.

<!--more-->

Down in the laugh den Joe Blow the chuckle chicken is doing his latest comedy gig. But he forgot the punchlines! Help them out and win over the crowd in this short comedy game.

Drag and drop the correct answers to the blank space.
